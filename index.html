Privacy Policy

CertCop is a maritime certificate and sea service tracking app by Dlyaver Apps.

Data stored in the app

CertCop may store the information you enter into the app, including profile details, certificate details, sea service records, current contract/status details, reminder settings, and attached certificate scans if you add them.

Local storage

Your app data is stored locally on your device. Certificate scans and profile photos, if added, remain stored locally on your device.

iCloud Backup

If you use iCloud Backup inside CertCop, the app may back up app data to your private iCloud account.

iCloud Backup includes app data only. Certificate scans, profile photos, generated PDFs, widget snapshots, and local file paths are not included.

Dlyaver Apps does not have access to your private iCloud data.

Data sharing

CertCop does not sell your data and does not share your personal app data with third parties.

Internet and third-party services

CertCop uses Apple iCloud/CloudKit only when you choose to use iCloud Backup.

The app does not use third-party analytics or advertising services.

User control

You control the data you enter into CertCop. You can delete app data from your device by deleting records inside the app or removing the app from your device.
